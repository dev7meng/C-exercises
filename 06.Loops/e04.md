Which one is not equivalent to the other two?
1. `for (i = 0; i < 10; i++);`
2. `for (i = 0; i < 10; ++i);`
3. `for (i = 0; i++ < 10; );

##### Answer:
Statement number 3 is difference to the other two

##### Explanation:
The statement 3 increase `i` before process to the loop body while the other two process after
